## TGA Compliance Essentials

### What the Agent Ensures

#### ‚úÖ Allowed
- General health information (educational)
- Service descriptions (what pharmacy offers)
- ARTG-listed product claims (as approved)
- Process explanations (how services work)
- Benefit statements (patient outcomes)
- Professional qualifications and credentials
- Location and contact information
- Pricing transparency

#### ‚ùå Not Allowed
- Cure claims for serious diseases
- Unapproved therapeutic claims
- Schedule 4/8 medication promotion to consumers
- Misleading or deceptive statements
- Testimonials implying guaranteed outcomes
- Comparison claims without substantiation
- Fear-based marketing (health scares)

### Required Disclaimers

The agent includes appropriate disclaimers:

**General Medical Advice**:
```
This information is general in nature and does not constitute medical advice.
Always consult with a qualified pharmacist or healthcare professional for
advice specific to your circumstances.
```

**Product Information**:
```
Always read the label. Follow the directions for use. If symptoms persist,
talk to your health professional. Vitamins and supplements can only be of
assistance if dietary intake is inadequate.
```

**Compounding Services**:
```
Compounded medications are prepared to individual specifications and may differ
from commercially available products. All compounding is performed in accordance
with TGA and Pharmacy Board of Australia standards.
```

```json

  "@type": "Pharmacy",
  "name": "[Pharmacy Name]",
  "image": "[Logo URL]",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "streetAddress": "[Street]",
    "addressLocality": "[Suburb]",
    "addressRegion": "NSW",
    "postalCode": "[Postcode]",
    "addressCountry": "AU"
  },
  "telephone": "[Phone]",
  "priceRange": "$$",
  "paymentAccepted": "Cash, Card, EFTPOS, Medicare"
}
```

### Service
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "Service",
  "serviceType": "Medication Review",

    "@type": "Pharmacy",

  },

  }
```
